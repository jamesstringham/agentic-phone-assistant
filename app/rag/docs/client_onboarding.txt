Stringham Consulting Services client onboarding guide.

New client consultation:
A new client consultation is designed to understand the client’s situation, goals, and next steps. The consultant may recommend a specific service after the first conversation.

What new clients should prepare:
- Full name and preferred contact information
- Short summary of what they need help with
- Relevant financial or business documents
- Any urgent deadlines
- Questions they want answered

For personal financial review:
Clients should bring or prepare:
- Monthly income estimate
- Monthly expenses
- Debt balances
- Savings goals
- Recent financial statements if available

For small business financial review:
Clients should bring or prepare:
- Revenue and expense records
- Profit and loss statement if available
- Bookkeeping reports
- Recent invoices or payment records
- List of current business concerns

For business strategy consultation:
Clients should bring or prepare:
- Business goals
- Current challenges
- Description of products or services
- Target customers
- Key decisions they are considering

For document review:
Clients should bring the documents they want reviewed. Ava should remind callers that consultants can explain and organize information, but may not be able to complete legal or tax filings.

What happens after the first appointment:
The consultant may recommend follow-up meetings, additional document review, a planning session, or a written action plan.

Client expectations:
Stringham Consulting Services focuses on clear explanations, practical next steps, and realistic planning.
