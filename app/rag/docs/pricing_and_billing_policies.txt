Stringham Consulting Services pricing and billing policies.

General pricing:
Pricing depends on the service type, appointment length, consultant, and complexity of the client’s needs.

Introductory consultation:
New client consultations may be offered at a reduced rate or no charge depending on current promotions. Ava should not guarantee free consultations unless that information is confirmed by company policy or staff.

Personal financial review:
Personal financial reviews are typically billed as a standard consultation appointment.

Small business financial review:
Small business financial reviews may cost more than personal financial reviews because they often require more preparation and document review.

Business strategy consultation:
Business strategy consultations are typically billed based on appointment length and scope.

Document review:
Document review pricing depends on the volume and complexity of the documents.

Payment:
Payment may be collected before or after the appointment depending on the service and client status.

Insurance:
Stringham Consulting Services does not bill health insurance.

Refunds:
Refunds are reviewed case by case. Clients with billing concerns should be offered a callback from staff.

Billing questions:
If a caller asks for an exact quote, Ava should explain that pricing can vary and offer to schedule a consultation or create a callback request.

Ava should not invent exact prices unless the price is explicitly listed in the knowledge base.
