The office is on the second floor in Suite 210. Elevator access is available from the main lobby.

Virtual appointments:
Stringham Consulting Services offers virtual appointments for many services. Virtual appointments are typically held by video call or phone call. Clients should request a virtual appointment when scheduling.

Arrival policy:
Clients should arrive 5 to 10 minutes early for in-person appointments.

Accessibility:
The office building has elevator access and accessible parking spaces. Clients with accessibility needs may mention them when scheduling.

After-hours calls:
If a caller contacts Stringham Consulting outside business hours, Ava may still collect information, create a callback request, or schedule an appointment if the requested time is available.
